ST. PAUL UNIVERSITY

St. Paul University Philippines is located in Tuguegarao City, Cagayan, Philippines. Founded in 1907 by the Sisters of St. Paul of Chartres, it became the first University in the Cagayan Valley Region in 1982. It offers basic, tertiary, professional, and graduate education.
