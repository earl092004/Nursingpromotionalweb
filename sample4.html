Why Choose Bishop Foley

Academic Excellence

Our rigorous college preparatory curriculum challenges students to achieve their highest potential through critical thinking, problem-solving, and effective communication skills.

Faith Formation

We nurture the spiritual growth of our students through prayer, worship, retreats, and service, fostering a deep understanding of Catholic teachings and values.

Community & Tradition

Our close-knit community fosters lifelong friendships and a sense of belonging, while our rich traditions connect students to our proud heritage and history.
