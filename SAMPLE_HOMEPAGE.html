OUR PHILOSOPHY

Our Mission

To develop competent, compassionate, and committed nurses through excellent, innovative education rooted in Paulinian values, serving humanity with utmost care and dedication.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of nursing excellence in the Philippines, forming globally competitive nurse professionals who embody Christian values and are dedicated to improving health outcomes.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion in patient care
  • Excellence in nursing practice
  • Integrity in all interactions
  • Service to the community

ST. PAUL UNIVERSITY
PHILIPPINES

St. Paul University Philippines is located in Tuguegarao City, Cagayan, Philippines. Founded in 1907 by the Sisters of St. Paul of Chartres, it became the first University in the Cagayan Valley Region in 1982. It offers basic, tertiary, professional, and graduate education.
