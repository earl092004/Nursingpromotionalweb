The Bachelor of Science in Nursing program at St. Paul University Philippines has a rich history of excellence in healthcare education. Over the years, our program has evolved to meet the changing demands of healthcare while maintaining our commitment to quality education.

Program Establishment

The Bachelor of Science in Nursing program was established at SPUP with a vision to develop competent, compassionate healthcare professionals guided by Paulinian values.

1990 Year Established
50 Initial Students

Program Expansion

The program expanded with new facilities, international partnerships, and enhanced curriculum to provide students with cutting-edge nursing education and global perspectives.

200+ Students Enrolled

National Recognition

SPUP's BSN program received Level IV Accreditation, the highest recognition from PACUCOA, affirming its status as one of the premier nursing programs in the Philippines.

Level IV Accreditation

ST. PAUL UNIVERSITY
PHILIPPINES

St. Paul University Philippines is located in Tuguegarao City, Cagayan, Philippines. Founded in 1907 by the Sisters of St. Paul of Chartres, it became the first University in the Cagayan Valley Region in 1982. It offers basic, tertiary, professional, and graduate education.
